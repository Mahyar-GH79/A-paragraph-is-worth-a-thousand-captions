An outdoor front view of a brick wall made up of nude colored bricks and white colored bricks, the bottom third of the wall is made up of only nude colored bricks, and the top two thirds of the wall is made up of white colored bricks. The wall consists of two rectangular shaped windows that have black borders and a vertical black metal beam going through the center, the windows are positioned vertically. Above the windows, in the center of both of them, is a black colored flood light that is attached to the brick wall, directly above the light are black colored letters that read,"TICKETS". The roof of the brick wall is an overhanging roof, and it provides shade to the top third of the wall, on the left side of the wall, to the left of both windows, is a silver water drain that starts at the ground floor, which is made up of cement, and runs all the way up the wall to the roof.
